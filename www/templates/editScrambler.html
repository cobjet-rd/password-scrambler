Edit Scrambler

The Scrambler is a mapping of characters of the service name to characters of the master password.

You can define which parts of the head or tail of the master password are replaced by parts of the head or tail of the service name.

Service Name
Minimum Length: {{scramblerSetup.headServiceParts.length + scramblerSetup.tailServiceParts.length}}

Master Password
Minimum Length: {{scramblerSetup.headMasterParts.length + scramblerSetup.tailMasterParts.length}}

The minim length describes the minimum length of the service name and master password, required by your Scrambler.
